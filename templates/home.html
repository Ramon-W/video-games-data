About:

This webapp contains video game data from The CORGIS Data Set Project, containing 1212 titles from the year 2004 to 2008. It includes general information about each video game, such as its publisher, sales, and review scores. The ESRB ratings that are used are E(veryone), T(eens), and M(ature). Review scores are from Metacritic.

Playtime statistics are crowdsourced from HowLongToBeat.com. This includes the average length (in hours) of all playstyles, as well the the overall average. If a playstyle for a game does not exist (e.g. completionists) or if there was no sufficient data, then it is denoted as 0.

The publisher of a game may be empty if the publisher is unkown. Special characters are omitted from the titles. There are a few errors with the dataset, such as having two copies of the same game.
