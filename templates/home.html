About:

This webapp contains video game data from The CORGIS Data Set Project, containing 1212 titles from the year 2004 to 2009. It includes the general information about each video game like its publisher, sales, and review scores.

One set of statistics for each video game is the playtime, crowdsourced from HowLongToBeat.com. This includes the average length (in hours) of all playstyles, as well as their averages. If a playstyle for a game does not exist (e.g. completionists), then it is denoted as 0. Additionally, if the game does not have a specific end or there is no sufficient data, it would also be denoted as 0.

Publisher may be left blank if the publisher of the video game is unknown. Ratings are from ESRB, but only includes E, T, and M. The review scores (out of 100) are from Metacritic.
